Privacy Policy

At Repro, we take privacy seriously and are committed to protecting our users' personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and share information when you use our product.

Personal Information:

The only personal information we collect is your name and email address. This information is used to create and manage your Repro account and to provide you with access to our product. We do not share this information with any third parties.

Website Data:

Any website data, network data, or other data captured during bug reporting is not shared with any third party, nor used for any purpose other than providing Repro's replayable developer tooling. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information related to website visitors or users.

Data Retention:

We retain website data, network data, and other captured data indefinitely to support our replayable developer tooling. However, we respect your right to privacy and can delete this data upon request.

Data Security:

We take appropriate measures to protect the security of your data and ensure that it is not lost, misused, or accessed without authorization. We use industry-standard security practices to safeguard your personal information and the data captured by our product.

Updates to Privacy Policy:

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on our website and will be effective immediately.

Age restrictions:

The extension and sharing app are not intended to be used by anyone under the age of 18.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or our use of your personal information, please contact us at privacy@repro.dev.
